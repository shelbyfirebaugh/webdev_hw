welcome

Like to read but have no money to spend on books? To lazy to wade through articles to find something interesting? Looking for fresh writing from up-and-coming authors? If you answered yes to any of the above, then you've come to the right place. Welcome to Lazy Literature, where you can find and read contemporary literature and poetry without clutter or hassle, anytime anywhere. So sit back, relax, and enjoy doing nothing but read for as long as possible. You're living ~the dream~.
